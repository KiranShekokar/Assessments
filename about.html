INSIGHTS OF ABC LIMITED


ABC India Limited has been a pioneer in the field of Logistics since its inception more than 49 years ago when there were hardly any other organized cargo Transporters in India. With this entire infrastructure ABC India Ltd is perfectly poised to provide effective solutions to every transportation need, from essential multi-modal, multi-location cargo handling to complicated Infrastucture projects. Overview Launched in 1962 as Surface Transport Operator Launched Project Division in 1986 to handle ODC/OWC with acquisition of Hydraulic Trailers & Heavy Duty Prime Movers Diversified into International Freight Forwarding in 1986 Registered as Multi Modal Transport Operator in 1995 (Road, Rail, Air and Water transport solutions) Formed JV with Nissin Corporation, Japan in 1999 to provide Japanese quality logistics services in India Today we are a Pan-India ISO 9001:2000 organization with 100 branches and a focussed expertise in value added Supply Chain Management service and end to end Project Logistics Resources Large Branch Network of 100 outlets in India including warehousing facilities with a total storage capacity of 200,000 Sq. meters Trained & Expperienced team strength exceeding 500 Web base ERP connecting all outlets for operations and accounts. Fleet strength of 250 owned trucks / trailers and 1000 attached Fleet of 60 axle lines Imported Goldhofer make and 108 axle lines Indian Tratec make Hydraulic Platform Trailers along with 15 prime movers and special equipments to transport ODC/OWC of up to 1200 tons 1900 ton IRC class coastal/inland waterways barge Member of IATA & FIATA with agents all over the world Multi Modal Transport Operator under Government Registration Having Own CHA Licences with exclusive arrangements for Freight forwarding activities at the major cities Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, New-Delhi and Kolkata Associated with reputed Freight Forwarders through out the world for international logistics services Associated with reputed Barge Operators for the transportation by inland water ways
